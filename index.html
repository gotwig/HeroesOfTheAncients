Heroes of the Ancients

Heroes of the Ancients is an upcoming Dota 2 mod, trying to bring the best ideas from Heroes of the Storm™ from Blizzard Entertainment™ to Valves Dota 2™!

Heroes of the Ancients is a team massive online battle arena. All teamplayer share the same level and its all about choosing the right abilities for each different map, making every fight unique. Fight together to capture Mercenary Camps, Watch Towers and face epic Dynamic Event Bosses to gain new allies and destroy the enemies ancient!

When will it be released?

When it's ready :) I started development in November 2015, and work since a few weeks all the time constantly on the mod.
